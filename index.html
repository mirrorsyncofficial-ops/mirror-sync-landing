Everything you need to know about Mirror Sync

Mirror Sync is completely non-custodial, meaning your funds NEVER leave your wallet. You maintain full control of your private keys at all times. Our smart contract only has permission to execute trades on your behalf - it cannot withdraw or transfer your funds. Additionally, our contract will undergo a professional security audit before mainnet launch.

Our AI system analyzes at least 6 months of on-chain trading history before approving any Guide. We look at win rate, risk management, consistency, maximum drawdown, and overall performance. Only traders who meet our strict criteria can register as Guides. All performance data is transparent and verifiable on-chain.

Mirror Sync charges a 0.1% platform fee on each trade (that's $1 per $1,000 traded). Guides earn a 10% performance fee, but ONLY on your profits - if you don't make money, they don't make money. There are no subscription fees, no monthly charges, and no hidden costs. Plus, Solana transaction fees average just $0.00025, making Mirror Sync 1000x cheaper than Ethereum-based alternatives.
