Everything you need to know about Mirror Sync

Mirror Sync is a decentralized copy trading platform on Solana that lets you automatically follow the trades of verified expert traders (Guides). Our AI verifies each Guide's trading history before they can accept followers.

Yes! Mirror Sync is completely non-custodial. Your funds never leave your wallet. You maintain full control of your private keys and can stop following a Guide at any time.

Our AI analyzes at least 6 months of on-chain trading history, including win rate, profit/loss ratio, maximum drawdown, and trading consistency. Only traders who meet our strict criteria can become Guides.

We charge a 0.1% platform fee on each trade plus a 10% performance fee on your profits (only when you make money). Guides also receive a 10% performance fee. This aligns everyone's incentives.
